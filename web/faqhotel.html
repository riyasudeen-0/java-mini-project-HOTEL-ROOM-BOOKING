Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a room booking on the website?

To book a room, simply: Select your room type. Choose your check-in and check-out dates. Select the number of guests and rooms. Click “Book Now” to proceed to the confirmation or payment page.

Can I modify or cancel my booking online?

Yes! Most bookings can be modified or canceled directly on the website before your check-in date. Please check the hotel’s cancellation policy for details, as some bookings may have restrictions.

Is my booking confirmed immediately?

Once your booking is successfully submitted, you will receive an instant confirmation via email or SMS. Make sure to save this confirmation for your records.

What payment methods are accepted for online booking?

The website accepts secure payment options, including: Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, etc.) Online banking Other digital payment methods (as listed during checkout) All transactions are encrypted to ensure your payment information is safe.
